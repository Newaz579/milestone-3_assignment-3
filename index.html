About Club

The Chittagong Club is one of the most prominent and prestigious social clubs in Bangladesh. Founded as a gentlemen’s club during British rule in 1878. In 1875, Mr. W.A Campbell, a well known Tea Planter of that period started a Club which was placed at the disposal of only the tea planters in order to provide logistic service during their stay in the district.

More Details About Club

The Club moved to Pioneer Hill the location of tea plantation founded in 1840. The land was rented to the club by Rai Nityananda Rai Bahadur, a Zamindar. On 27 March 1901, the club was opened at the new location. The club was registered as a company in 1908 .Predecessor: Provisional Club, Founded at: British India (now Chattogram) Formation: 1878

Some of the more popular amenities offered include free wifi, an on-site restaurant, and a lounge.
